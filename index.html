Future

We are the Company for Visualization & Simulation (CVS) for Research & Development of handcrafted 3D Simulations based on the newest technologies

mission

Our main task is R&D for 3D simulations, promoting IT applications for many sectors, especially for the Business, Education and Tourism & Hospitality Industries.

competence

CVS is competent in Project Management as well as Customer Services and capable of developing and managing large, long-term and complex home_portfolio.

experience

CVS develops many software and hardware applications and deploys home_portfolio related to simulation, and reconstruction using 3D Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality technology.

research

CVS actively researches solutions for modeling, reconstruction and re-creation of 3D objects, building 3d spaces, and simulations of interactive controls.

industry 4.0 focus

CVS is focused on researching and deploying the latest developments and solutions for 3D visualization, virtual reality, augmented reality, artifical intelligence and the Internet of Things, in answer to the needs of O.B.O.R for Vietnam and globally.

cutting-edge

CVS follows the latest technological developments for user interaction with objects in 3D space, through novel projection equipment, computers, control devices, and peripheral sensors to reate the most realistic, ergonomic and user-friendly experience for the user.
